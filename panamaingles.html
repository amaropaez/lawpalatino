WE CREATE YOUR COMPANY IN PANAMA

Opening a company in Panama can offer various benefits for entrepreneurs and companies.





ZERO TAXES.

Panama companies guarantee zero taxes when receiving profits outside of Panama. They enjoy of territorial and privileged taxation.

ANONYMOUS AND SECURE.

Easy to onboard, require minimal due diligence and are completely anonymous with the main candidates. They open remotely without your presence.

FOR GLOBAL AND VIRTUAL OPERATIONS.

Like all offshore companies, Panamanian companies are also prepared to carry out any activity lawful worldwide by law, preserving privacy.





Speed ​​and Simplicity in Incorporation.

The process of incorporating companies in Panama stands out for being quick and simple.
Low ongoing maintenance costs make it an attractive option for entrepreneurs and companies.

Common Uses of Panamanian Companies.

Panamanian companies are used to structure international taxes.
They are used in international commercial transactions, access to bank accounts and e-commerce.
They are also used in wealth management and as holding companies.

Compliance and Recordkeeping.

Panamanian companies have compliance obligations, including maintaining accounting records and underlying documentation.
These records must be available for at least 5 years.
They can be kept in the office of the resident agent in Panama or elsewhere determined by the board of directors.
